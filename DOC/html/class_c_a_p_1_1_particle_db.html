◆ mapAntiParticleIndices()

void CAP::ParticleDb::mapAntiParticleIndices ( )

Find the antiparticles of all particles in the DB and store their DB index for fast retrieval in some functions. (assumes no sorting is done after this operation) Antiparticles have -pdgCode of their particle and viceversa Particles that have no antiparticle will store -1 to indicates they do not have an anti-particle.
